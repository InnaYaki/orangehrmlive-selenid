О нас

At OrangeHRM our goal is to help HR do amazing work that they love. HR for All isn’t just a statement at OrangeHRM, it’s a stance that we took when the company was founded in 2006 to help our HR allies make a difference with powerful software tools. OrangeHRM provides a world-class HRIS experience and offers everything you and your team need to be that HR hero you know that you are.
